About VitalPeak

BUILT FOR YOUR BEST SELF

VitalPeak is a free, science-backed fitness and health platform designed to help individuals understand their bodies, track their progress, and achieve sustainable wellness goals — without a gym membership or personal trainer required.

Our Mission

We believe that accurate health information and powerful tracking tools shouldn't be locked behind paywalls. VitalPeak provides professional-grade calculators and guidance, completely free, to empower millions of people to take charge of their physical health and wellbeing.

The Science Behind Our Calculators

All calculations use validated medical formulas: the Mifflin-St Jeor equation for BMR, the US Navy circumference method for body fat, and multi-formula averaging for ideal weight. Our team continuously reviews the latest peer-reviewed research to keep everything current and accurate.
